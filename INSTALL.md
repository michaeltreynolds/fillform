# Installing FillForm in Chrome

FillForm is a small Chrome add-on that helps fill out FamilySearch's
"Add Person" and "Add Parent" forms from your pasted data. It's installed by
hand (it's not in the Chrome Web Store), which takes about two minutes.

You only do steps 1–6 once.

---

## 1. Download the add-on

Click this link and save the file:

**https://github.com/michaeltreynolds/fillform/releases/latest/download/fillform.zip**

It downloads a file called **fillform.zip**.

## 2. Unzip it to a folder you'll keep

Find **fillform.zip** (usually in your Downloads), right-click it, and choose
**Extract All…** (Windows) or double-click it (Mac).

> **Important:** move the unzipped **fillform** folder somewhere permanent —
> for example your **Documents** folder. Chrome runs the add-on directly from
> this folder, so if you delete or move it later, the add-on stops working.

## 3. Open Chrome's Extensions page

In Chrome, click the address bar, type this, and press Enter:

```
chrome://extensions
```

## 4. Turn on "Developer mode"

Flip the **Developer mode** switch in the **top-right** corner to **on**.

## 5. Click "Load unpacked"

A few buttons appear on the top-left. Click **Load unpacked**.

## 6. Choose the fillform folder

In the window that opens, select the **fillform** folder you unzipped in step 2,
and click **Select Folder**.

That's it — a **FillForm** card appears on the page. ✅

---

## Pin it so you can find it

Click the little puzzle-piece icon near the top-right of Chrome, then click the
pin next to **FillForm**. Its icon now stays on your toolbar.

## Using it

1. Click the **FillForm** toolbar icon and paste your data, then **Load records**.
2. Go to FamilySearch and open **Add Unconnected Person** — a small FillForm
   panel appears with a **Fill** button.
3. Click **Fill**, then search and decide as usual. You're always in control;
   it only fills the boxes.
4. After creating a person, use **Add Parent** — the panel offers
   **Fill Father / Fill Mother**.
5. Click **Next ▶** in the panel to move to the next record.

---

## Getting a newer version later

1. Download the zip again from the link in step 1.
2. Unzip it and **replace** your existing fillform folder with the new one
   (same location).
3. Go to `chrome://extensions` and click the **reload** (↻) icon on the
   FillForm card.

You can check which version you have at the bottom of the FillForm popup.

## Things you might see (all normal)

- Chrome may show a note like *"Disable developer-mode extensions."* You can
  click **Cancel** / close it — FillForm needs developer mode to run, and it's
  safe to leave on.
- If the icon disappears, it's just unpinned — redo the **Pin it** step above.
